## 3-Step Setup

### 1. Get your API Token

Go to [@BotFather](https://t.me/botfather) on Telegram and get your **API Token**.

### 2. Sync & Demo

Join our [Telegram Group](https://t.me/slopification) for a "Live Demo." We will manually sync your token with our engine.

- The bot must be added as an **Admin** in your group.
- A 10-minute live demo is activated immediately.

### 3. Unlock Credits

Once the demo ends, contact [@itsbullishnow](https://t.me/itsbullishnow) to unlock your generation credits.

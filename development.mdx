### `/slop [your idea]`

The main command for the [Slop AI Bot](https://t.me/slopai_bot). Simply describe what you want the mascot to do, and the AI will forge the visual.

- **Example:** `/slop eating dominos pizza in front of Fortnite`

## For Admins

### Customization

The bot output is fully customizable (with additional costs). You can add these branding layers:

- **Project Watermarks**: Automatic logo overlay on every meme.
- **Custom Overlays**: Add your ticker or social handles.
- **Smart Text**: Specific dynamic text on generated images.
